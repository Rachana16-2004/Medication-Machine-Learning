About the Developers

Meet the Creators

This project was proudly developed by Rachana and Preethika, Computer Science students passionate about using technology for real-world impact.

Our Journey

As students, we always believed in the power of learning by doing. This web application is the result of our shared curiosity in Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, and Health Technology. It began as a project for academic purposes, but we hope it becomes a helpful tool for many.

Why We Built This

We noticed how difficult it can be for people to understand their symptoms or get quick health insights. So we built a platform that uses AI and a simple interface to help users get an idea of potential health issues — all through technology and research we’ve learned during our studies.

Our Goals

Our goal is to not only complete a project but to create something meaningful. We wanted to apply our classroom knowledge in machine learning, data analysis, and frontend development into something that can contribute to people’s well-being.

A Note from Us

Thank you for visiting our project! We’re always learning and growing, and we hope to keep building more tools that make a difference. If you have feedback, suggestions, or just want to say hi — feel free to connect with us!
